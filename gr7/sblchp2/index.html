Quick Revision: Sensors detect inputs from the environment and convert them into electrical or digital signals. Temperature sensors measure temperature, ultrasonic sensors measure distance using sound waves, light sensors detect light levels and motion sensors detect movement. A 16x2 LCD displays 16 characters on each of two lines. In an I2C LCD connection, GND connects to GND, VCC to 5V, SDA to A4 and SCL to A5 on Arduino Uno. In the temperature sensor circuit, Vout connects to A1.
