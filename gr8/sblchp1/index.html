Quick Revision: Intelligence is the ability to understand, learn and think. Human intelligence combines Stimulus → Sensing → Reasoning → Response. Ability to reason makes a human intelligent. Machines sense through sensors and scanners. The term Artificial Intelligence was first coined by John McCarthy in 1956. AI is the art and science of developing machines running on intelligent algorithms. PictoBlox is a programming environment used to write scripts for AI projects.
