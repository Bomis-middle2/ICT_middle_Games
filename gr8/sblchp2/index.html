Quick Revision: A chatbot is an AI-driven computer program that simulates human-like conversation through voice commands or text. Google Assistant, Siri and Alexa are examples of conversational AI. Rule-based chatbots use if/then logic with pre-defined question and answer combinations (like FAQ bots). AI-powered chatbots use Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand and generate human-like responses, and they learn from interactions over time.
